INSTITUTION ROLES
1. Principal (or Rector / President)
Role: Overall head of the college, responsible for academic and administrative oversight.

2. Vice Principals / Deputy Principals
Examples:
Vice Principal: Academic
Vice Principal: Administration & Finance
Vice Principal: Student Affairs
3. Deans of Faculties / Schools
Oversee specific academic areas.
4. Heads of Departments (HoDs)
Oversee academic departments like Science, Arts, Business, etc.

5. Academic Staff
Professors / Associate Professors / Senior Lecturers
Lecturers / Assistant Lecturers / Tutors

6. Administrative Staff (Non-Academic, Managerial)
a. Registrar
Handles student records, admissions, examinations.
b. Human Resources Manager
Manages staffing, contracts, payroll coordination.
c. ICT Manager
Manages technology infrastructure and e-learning platforms.
d. Librarian
Oversees academic resource management.

✅ e. Accounting and Finance Team
Reports typically to the Bursar, Chief Financial Officer (CFO), or Vice Principal for Administration & Finance.

Role	Responsibilities
Bursar / CFO	Senior finance officer; oversees all financial matters. Prepares budgets, financial statements, ensures regulatory compliance.
Accountants	Maintain financial records, prepare reports, manage reconciliations, track expenditures, assist in budgeting.
Finance Officers / Analysts	Assist in budgeting, auditing, forecasting, and financial planning. May liaise with departments.
Payroll Officer	Manages salaries, deductions, benefits, and compliance with tax/labor regulations.
Accounts Payable / Receivable Clerks	Process incoming and outgoing payments, handle invoices, maintain vendor/student accounts.
Cashiers	Handle physical or electronic receipts and disbursements, fee collection, and petty cash.
Internal Auditor (if applicable)	Monitors financial systems and ensures compliance with policies and internal controls.

7. Support and Service Staff (Non-Academic, Operational)
Clerks / Secretaries
Technicians / Lab Assistants
Catering Staff / Cleaners
Security Personnel
Drivers / Transport Staff
Groundskeepers / Maintenance
